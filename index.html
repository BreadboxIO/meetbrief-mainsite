Step 1

Join or Create a meeting

Meetbrief pops up as soon as you join a meeting at meet.google.com. Simple enable the extension and it'll handle the rest.

Step 2

Enable Closed Captions

Our transcription requires closed captions to be enabled. Meetbrief then begins recording all of the conversation live.

  • Real-time speaker diarization
  • Captions are only enabled for you
Step 3

Save your meeting transcript

Instantly save the transcript by clicking on the extension icon in the browser. Private and secure, only stored in your browser.

Helpful answers

Anything and everything you've always wondered about meetbrief.

How much does meetbrief cost?

Nadda, zero, zip - It's free to use (for now). We may be adding in paid-only features, we may just release 'em free. Who knows.

Can I auto-install this for others on my team?

To install for all of your users, contact your GSuite admin. Information on how to do this can be found here

Do you have versions for other meeting tools (Zoom, etc)?

You've spoken, we've listened - of course we'd love meetbrief to handle all the major meeting tools and conferencing providers. While we've focused our attention on projects for the time being, if you really want us to, though, give us a shout, we'd love to chat.

How secure is meetbrief's transcription?

We don't transmit or store any meeting information anywhere but on your browser. It's yours, why would we try and compromise that?

How do I access my meeting transcription?

Click the icon in your meeting to show/hide the live-feed transcription process. In order to export and save your transcription, just click the button "Download".

Will everyone in the meeting see the transcriptions?

Nope! They aren't even notified whether or not you're showing captions either. The transcription will only show for you, but contain the entire meeting's conversation.
